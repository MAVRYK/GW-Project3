Created by Vanessa Van Gilder, Spencer Rand, Kellie Shontz,
Anna Riehle, Mike Hager, and Yegor Kryukov


Lexicography

This word cloud displays the words that show up the most often across every site we looked at. Words that appear more frequently are larger than words that don't appear as often.



Crossing the Divide

This chord diagram shows the connectedness between sites. Each arc represents an instance of one site linking to another; the thicker the arc, the greater the number of links.

Washington Post and Politico are two sites that others frequently link to. Hovering over the edge of the circle for either of those sites, you'll see a number of "thick" bands coming from other sites
